Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Occelium ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Occelium mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you create an account and use our app:

  • Email address (for account creation and authentication)
  • Job preferences (positions, contract types, skills, location)
  • Saved job offers and application tracking data
  • Notification preferences
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging token (for push notifications)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Personalize your job feed based on your preferences
  • Send you daily job alert notifications
  • Improve the app and fix issues

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Firebase (Google) — for push notifications
  • Resend — for transactional emails (password reset, email verification)

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at contact@occelium.com.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Children's Privacy

Occelium is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
